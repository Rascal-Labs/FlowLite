The easiest way to add them is to drag the two buttons above to your bookmarks bar. Drag to register them; do not click them here.

Easiest setup

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + B to show the bookmarks bar.
  2. Drag FlowLite Request Form to the bookmarks bar.
  3. Drag Open in FlowLite to the bookmarks bar.
  4. After that, use them as buttons on your bookmarks bar.

How to use

  1. Click FlowLite Request Form and enter the relationship diagram you want.
  2. The form copies a prompt that includes the FlowLite specification.
  3. Paste it into an AI input field and generate FlowLite plain text.
  4. Copy the generated FlowLite plain text, then click Open in FlowLite.

Automatic one-click bookmark installation is not available

Ordinary web pages cannot automatically add bookmarklets to your browser. Browser security restrictions make drag registration the simplest and most robust method.

If dragging does not work

Right-click the link, copy its URL, create a new bookmark, and paste it into the URL field. On desktop, dragging is still the easiest method.
